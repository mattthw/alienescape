///////////
HOW TO PLAY
///////////

START:
    press left or right to change difficulty
    press 'A', or 'Start' to begin.
GAME LEVEL:
    Goal: avoid the thingamajigs flying right
        at you! You must get the alien to the
        end of the screen so that he may reach
        his ship to fly away! Fail at this
        and his kin may just KILL ALL HUMANS!
    Controls:
        Press left or right to move in that
        direction.
        Press 'Up' or 'A' to jump.
        Press 'Select' to return to START.
    Gameplay:
        Try to reach the end of the screen.
        If one of the yellow bullets hits you
        then you will lose 33% of your health
        and be reset to the starting position.
        Get hit 3 times and it's GAME OVER.
GAME OVER:
    press start, select, or A to continue
WIN SCREEN:
    press start, select, or A to continue
